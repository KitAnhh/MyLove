Our Story

🪇We are a friendly community founded in 2023. Whether you play guitar, piano, drums, or just love listening — everyone is welcome here

👯‍♂️Initially, the club was founded by just four people with a shared interest, but today it has over 100 members with diverse talents.

🕺The club will organize instrumental and singing performances and open up opportunities for musical interaction, helping people connect through music.
